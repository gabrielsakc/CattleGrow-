Our Vision

The operating system for modern cattle operations

CattleGrow connects sensors, RFID identification, livestock intelligence, feeding systems, CRM, ERP, and AI into one real-time platform—giving dairy farms, feedlots, and livestock operations the visibility, control, and business intelligence to make better decisions every day.

Modern cattle operations are managing far more than animals. They are managing feeding, identification, workflows, inventory, suppliers, customers, and business performance.

CattleGrow brings all of these layers together in one connected ecosystem.

Through advanced engineering, proprietary technology, sensors, and RFID-based identification, the platform captures real-time information directly from the field and transforms it into clear, actionable intelligence. Every animal can be identified. Every key process can be tracked.

But CattleGrow goes beyond monitoring. It also functions as a complete CRM and ERP for the operation, connecting livestock data with business workflows, supplier coordination, and human resources. This creates a single source of truth across the entire business.

The platform also supports online diet systems and connected nutrition workflows, allowing producers and specialists to manage feeding strategies with greater precision, consistency, and speed.

With AI helping reveal patterns and surface insights, CattleGrow turns complexity into clarity and real-time data into meaningful action—one platform, one ecosystem, and one real-time view of the entire operation.

Intelligent monitoring for more efficient livestock management

Smart CattleGrow®
Collar Intelligence.

The Smart CattleGrow® Collar is an advanced monitoring system designed to improve animal health, productivity, and security through intelligent sensors, GPS tracking, and real-time data analysis.

Feature CattleGrow® Collar Traditional Systems
Health Tracking Rumination, Posture, Temp, Activity Limited or Visual only
GPS tracking Real-time + Geofencing None or Manual
Early Detection AI Alerts for Stress/Lame/Heat Reactive detection

The collar continuously monitors activity, rumination, posture, body temperature, and movement patterns, enabling early detection of diseases, stress, or locomotion problems such as lameness. Its advanced algorithms also support accurate heat detection and reproductive management.

Unlike many traditional systems, the Smart CattleGrow® Collar includes real-time GPS tracking, allowing farmers to locate animals, establish geofences, and receive alerts for unusual movements or potential theft.

All data is integrated into a cloud-based platform accessible from mobile or desktop, using artificial intelligence to generate early alerts, predictive insights, and herd management reports.

The result is a powerful tool that enables producers to make data-driven decisions, improve animal welfare, and optimize farm productivity.

Precision Nutrition & Efficient Logisitics

Feeding Management.

Our Feeding Management system optimizes the entire feeding process to ensure accuracy, efficiency, and optimal animal nutrition.

Ration & Diet Formulation

Create and manage precise feeding rations by adjusting both dry matter (DM) and as-fed values, ensuring accurate nutrient delivery. Water can also be incorporated as a separate ingredient when required for specific diet formulations.

Bunk Reading & Intake Adjustment

The system records bunk readings to estimate feed refusals and automatically adjusts the next day’s feed deliveries, helping maintain consistent intake while minimizing waste.

Delivery Planning

Plan and schedule multiple feed deliveries per pen per day, improving feed availability, animal performance, and operational efficiency.

Mixer Integration for Operators

A dedicated operator interface for feed mixers displays target vs. actual ingredient loading and mixing times, ensuring precise batching, proper mixing, and full traceability during feed preparation.

This integrated approach improves feeding precision, reduces waste, and enhances overall herd productivity.

Comprehensive Traceability & Veterinary Care

Animal Health Management.

Our Animal Health Management system provides a comprehensive and traceable approach to veterinary care and disease control.

Diagnosis-Based Health Protocols

Link medical protocols directly to specific diagnoses (pneumonia, anemia, etc.), standardizing care with clear treatment descriptions and instructions.

Individual & Group Treatments

Automatic dose calculations based on weight or head count, reducing human error for both solo and group medication.

Flexible Sick Management

Manage animals in their original pens or hospital pens with real-time capacity tracking and logistics support.

Complete Health Records & Analytics

Traceable animal profiles for temperatures and diagnoses, powered by a centralized dashboard with morbidity and mortality analytics.

Integrated Health Ecosystem

Connects directly with chute processing and pharmaceutical inventory for automatic stock and cost updates.

Withdrawal & Data Transfer

Strict regulatory withdrawal control and secure data exchange for uninterrupted traceability across the supply chain.

This system improves animal welfare, treatment accuracy, regulatory compliance, and operational efficiency across modern livestock operations.

Lightning-Fast Processing at the Chute

High-Efficiency
Chute Hub.

Chute & Processing is the critical execution point, connecting physical cattle handling with the platform’s health, inventory, and financial management.

Hardware-Agnostic Data Capture

Seamless integration via Bluetooth with industry-standard scale heads (Digi-Star, Weigh-Tronix, etc.) and RFID readers (Gallagher, Tru-Test, etc.) for automatic, error-free weight and EID capture.

Maximum Speed Workflows

Receive cattle, assign EIDs, and apply protocols in one flow. "Quick Start" mode allows instant profile access and treatment logging on the fly.

Automated Sorting

Draft animals automatically into pens based on weight ranges or headcount. Manual overrides allow sorting by sex or body condition.

Continuous-Flow UI

Designed for high-throughput; the system automatically advances to the next animal after capture, enabling uninterrupted processing sessions.

Full Ecosystem Integration

Treatments update health records, deduction from pharma inventory occurs auto-calculated by weight, and costs hit the financial ledger instantly.

This seamless integration transforms the chute into a high-speed data capture hub, ensuring every action instantly updates operational, health, and financial intelligence.

Dynamic Inventory Pricing & Ration Integration

Inventory
& Ration Pricing.

Cattler’s Inventory system connects feed ingredients, veterinary supplies, and financial operations into a single dynamic platform where every movement impacts costs in real time.

Dynamic Ingredient Pricing

Configure NEM, NEG, and DM parameters. Weighted average costs automatically update when new stock arrives, ensuring precise valuation of combined inventory.

Real-Time Ration Impact

Purchasing price changes instantly propagate through all active rations, providing managers with immediate visibility into current feeding economics.

Premixes & Feed Manufacturing

Digitally mirror on-site feed mills. The system deducts raw materials, applies mixing shrink, and creates new premix balances with full traceability.

Total Financial Control & Analytics

Billing & Financial
Intelligence.

Automated Billing & Grain Banks

Generate invoices based on exact consumption, yardage, and health treatments. Track bulk grain deposits and convert them into credits for client billing.

Real-Time Economics & Closeouts

Calculate precise FCR, cost of gain, and daily cost per head. Every transaction flows into real-time closeout reports for a complete operation picture.

Enterprise Financial Sync

Direct QuickBooks integration for all invoices, with active support for ERP systems like SAP and Finnegans to maintain professional financial ledgers.
